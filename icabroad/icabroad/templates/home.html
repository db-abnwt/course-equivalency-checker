Welcome!

You will explore the list of partner universities available for your study abroad application and also the courses that had been approved from the previous years. The course equivalency in this database is based on the previous batches of MUIC outbound exchange students only. You should refer to the host university website for the list of courses offered in the particular semester that you are intended to study abroad. Please note that courses are subject to the host university’s availability in each semester/year. The student exchange office will guide you through proceed your own courses/study plan equivalency prior to your study dates at the host university.

Advantages of being an exchange student

An exchange student is who, by prior formal arrangement, undertakes short-term study (one term to one academic year) at one of the MUIC’s partner universities (under a reciprocal basis). Students will pay tuition fee at MUIC for their exchange abroad while tuition fee at the host university will be waived. Credits earned during the semester abroad will be transferred to MUIC academic records (subject to the result of course equivalency arrangement).
